Cinematic Spectator Mod for Unreal Tournament 2004

A mutator/gametype that replaces the default spectator camera with a smooth, cinematic follow‑cam. 
The camera intelligently tracks players, lingers on the killer after a frag, and automatically switches between targets based on score.

Features

Smooth camera movement – interpolation for both location and rotation.
Frag linger – after a frag, the camera stays on the perpetrator for a configurable duration.
Auto target switching – cycles through alive players based on their score (or random if scores are equal).
Works with both human players and bots.

Installation

1. Download the latest release, or compile the source yourself.
2. Place the `.u` files into your UT2004 `System` folder.
3. Enjoy!
